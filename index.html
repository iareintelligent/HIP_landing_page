Watkins Mill Town Center

At its heart of the Spectrum is Performers Park, which features a large splash pad fountain and a permanent stage for performances and events. Spectrum's residential component includes the 224-unit Paramount East and West apartments and the Majestic, a 243-unit luxury apartment with both public and gated residential parking. The Spectrum is part of the larger Watkins Mill Town Center, slated to include 4.5 million square feet of Class A office, hotel, restaurants, retail and more than 2,200 homes and apartment units.

Paramount West

Providing the architectural edge of Performers Park, Paramount West is a multi-use apartment building located in the heart of The Spectrum. Paramount West will include ground level restaurants and will stimulate the environment within Performers Park and the restaurant space within Paramount will utilize outdoor seating on the park.

Performer's Park

Performers Park will provide an exciting place for the community to come and be entertained. A splash pad will provide outdoor fun for children and a stage with programmed events will provide entertainment for people of all ages. The park will be used for large community events and shows.

Paramount East

Paramount East is a large apartment building located directly across from Performers Park. Paramount East provides an amenity rich experience for the residence as well as ground level retail for residents and visitors alike.

The Majestic

The largest apartment building within Spectrum, the majestic includes three outdoor terraces and a plethora of indoor amenity space overlooking Performers Park. The Majestic includes ground level retail and restaurant units.

Barking Mad Cafe

With 100+ outdoor seats that overlook Performer's Park, Barking Mad Cafe offers super premium coffee and scratch-baked sweets and savories Pizzas are cooked in a wood hearth, farm-to-table food is paired with specialty cocktails and house infusions. A number of ingredients are grown on-site with our aeroponic, organic garden where we grow vegetables, herbs and edible flowers.
